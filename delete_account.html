How to Delete Your Chibox Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the Chibox mobile application. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Open the Chibox App
    Launch the Chibox application on your mobile device
  2. Go to Your Profile
    Tap on the "Profile" icon in the bottom navigation bar
  3. Scroll Down
    Scroll to the bottom of your profile page
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
    Find and tap the red "Delete Account" button at the bottom of the page
  5. Confirm Deletion
    Read the confirmation message and tap "Delete" to confirm (Note: This action cannot be undone)
